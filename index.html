Our Speciality

Kani Sushi

Kani sushi is a crab sushi dish with rice wrapped in seaweed. It's served with soy sauce and wasabi. Sushi is nutritious, providing protein, omega-three fatty acids, and vitamins A and B12.

California Maki

Uramakizushi with cucumber, crab or imitation crab, and avocado contains 255 calories. It has 7 grams of mostly healthy monounsaturated fat from avocado. The roll provides 9 grams of protein from crab meat, it is actually fish.

Salmon Sashimi

sashimi is a raw fish delicacy served with sauce and garnishes. It's both delicious and healthy when prepared properly. The salmon must be safe for raw consumption. Sashimi can be enjoyed without cooking. Enjoy this delectable dish.

Tuna Sashimi

Tuna sashimi is a popular delicacy made from thinly sliced tuna paired with condiments and garnishes. It offers a rich source of omega-3 fatty acids, iron, B vitamins, potassium, and selenium, providing several health benefits. Sashimi often includes fresh and nutritious vegetables as well.
